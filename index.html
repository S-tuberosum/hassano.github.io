About me

My name is Omar and I'm a graduate student specializing in physics at the University of Victoria. I hope you can learn more about me and my research through this site.

One of my main interests is applying machine learning techniques to particle/nuclear physics problems, which started during my bachelor's and continues into my master's degree.

The project I'm currently focused on relates to automating beam tuning routines, with close application to the DRAGON experiment. My supervisors are Oliver Kester and J. Michael Roney. More details can be found in my research section.
